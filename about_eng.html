Brand Journey

Key Moments in History

1881
The brand was founded in the town of Sealy, Texas. The company's name comes from the town's name.
20th Century
Sealy becomes one of the leading mattress brands in the US, expanding into international markets.
Today
Sealy is present in 50+ countries worldwide. The brand is chosen by The White House and leading luxury hotels, including Ritz-Carlton.
10 Years in Almaty
Ritz-Carlton Almaty has used Sealy mattresses for over 10 years — a local proof point of the brand's world-class quality.
